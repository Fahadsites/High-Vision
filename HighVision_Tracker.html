Hook — Problem Based
"Hey [Name] — I found something on your page that's costing you clients every single day. Give me 60 seconds before you click off."
Intro + Credibility
"I'm Fahad. I've been working with coaches — helping them get the right people onto their page, and then converting those right people into paying clients. Before I get into what I'd build for you — let me show you some of the results we've been getting."
👆 Show screenshots on screen here
Diagnosis
"So I went through your last few videos — you're getting around [X] views. That's actually not bad — that's not the problem. The problem is what happens after someone watches. They go to your bio, they see a link, they click it — and now they're on a website that has to do all the heavy lifting to get them on a call. Most people just leave. What you're missing is a system that turns those viewers into booked calls automatically."
Step 1 — Attention
"Phase one is attention. We reverse engineer the best performing videos in your niche, find what's working for your top competitors, and model that into your own content. On top of that we run profile visit ads so the right people are landing on your page every single day — not random viewers, your exact target audience. Your profile becomes a filter."
Step 2 — Nurture
"Phase two is nurture. We create a VSL — a Video Sales Letter — a short video showing who you are, who you help, and the results you get. We host that on YouTube and model it around what's already working in your niche. That YouTube link goes straight into your bio. So instead of someone bouncing off your profile after 60 seconds — they click that link and spend 45 minutes watching your content. By the time they reach your booking page they already know you, they already trust you, and they're basically pre-sold."
Step 3 — Convert
"Phase three is conversion. Every new follower that comes in gets reached out to by our appointment setter. A setter is someone on my team whose only job is to reach out to your new followers, start a conversation, qualify the ones who are ready to buy, and book them straight onto your calendar. You don't spend a minute in the DMs. You just show up to calls with people who are already ready."
CTA
"I've got 2-3 specific ideas mapped out for your account — I'd rather walk you through them on a quick call so I can tailor it exactly to where you're at. Takes 15 mins. My calendar will pop up after this video ends — you can book directly from there. Speak soon."
